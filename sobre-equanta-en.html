Business based on regenerative systems and sustainability

Environmental Studies for Biodiversity Conservation and Human Quality of Life

We are eQuanta, an environmental consulting firm that offers solutions for your business, combining technical rigor and cost-effectiveness in relationships of cooperation, transparency and synergy. We form a multidisciplinary technical team, organized according to the characteristics of each enterprise. We serve organizations, governments and private companies interested in valuing their businesses through sustainability.



Ethics

Commitment to sustainability, based on research and application of knowledge together with consolidated methodologies, to align your project with nature conservation.

Dialogue

From the first contact, you negotiate directly with those who plan your environmental project, based on frank and objective dialogue.

Gain

With emphasis on the specific needs of the enterprise, we design the best possible solution: lean and objective, aiming to generate value through biodiversity conservation.

Latent Interests

Optimization of forest management for recovery projects; ecological techniques and metrics in the industrial scope, based on energy efficiency; mariculture systems through a socio-ecological approach based on reducing impacts on the functional and scenic aspects of nature; new environmental data analysis tools.

Ecology & Responsibility

We mediate intervention in natural ecosystems supported by the triad ecology-distributed responsibility-business, and produce elements for decision-making in enterprises that interfere with the dynamics of terrestrial and aquatic natural ecosystems.

Our Scope

In terrestrial ecosystems we provide services for real estate ventures, industries and rural producers. In aquatic ecosystems we serve the Oil and Gas, Ports and Marinas industries, as well as governments and Third Sector organizations.

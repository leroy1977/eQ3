Aquatic Environmental Studies


We offer specialized ecological consultancy focused on coastal, marine, and estuarine environments. Our expertise supports project planning, licensing, environmental protection, and sustainable management of ecological and economic resources.

Our Services:

  • Environmental monitoring for medium and large-scale projects;

  • Rapid Ecological Assessment and Preliminary Environmental Reports (REA/PER);

  • Environmental Impact Studies and Reports (EIS/EIR) for physical and biological environments;

  • Basic Environmental Projects (BEP), emphasizing physical and biotic environments and focusing ecosystems;

  • Methodological Design for for Optimized Responses and Critical Analysis;

  • Remote sensing and Geospatial Analysis (GIS);

  • Strategic Planning for Sensitive Areas and Biodiversity Protection;

  • Collection and Analysis of Biotic and Abiotic Data;

  • Meteoceanographic Data Analysis and Modeling;

  • Rapid Biodiversity Assessment / Inventories (RBA/RBI);

  • Rapid Assessment by Visual Expedition (RAVE);

  • Preparation of Expert Reports and Critical Analyses.


Aquatic Biota Monitoring Programs:

  • Monitoring in Coastal Ecosystems - Mangroves, Rocky Shores, Sandy Beaches, and Coral Reefs;

  • Invasive Species Monitoring;

  • Biometry Studies;

  • Phytoplankton and Zooplankton Monitoring;

  • Ichthyofauna Monitoring;

  • Seabird Monitoring;

  • Benthic and Soft-Bottom Ecosystems Monitoring.

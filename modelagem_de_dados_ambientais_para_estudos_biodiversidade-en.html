Environmental Data Analysis and Modeling


At eQuanta, we develop and apply quantitative analytical tools in the fields of geoinformation, remote sensing, and environmental data modeling. Our goal is to enhance the understanding of environmental factors that condition impacts on ecosystems and to support decision-making processes for biodiversity conservation and the maintenance of ecological functions.
Environmental data analysis plays a central role in Environmental Monitoring Studies, where it improves the accuracy and interpretability of environmental responses — resulting in more efficient, evidence-based insights for environmental management and sustainable project planning.

Services Offered:

  • Processing, Systematization, and Treatment of Primary and Secondary Qualitative-Quantitative Ecological Data

  • Analysis of Extreme and Anomalous Events for Coastal and Marine Infrastructure Projects

  • Exploratory, Inferential, and Modeling Analysis of Metoceanographic Data

  • Structuring and Publication of Environmental Data

Methodological Design


Sound environmental interpretation depends on the correct selection of variables and metrics, as well as on the robust application of analytical and modeling techniques. Our team supports Environmental Data Planning and Analysis activities, assisting in the design and adaptation of project scopes and environmental monitoring programs, with particular expertise in marine and coastal systems.



Additional Services:

  • Processing and Systematization of Preliminary Quali-Quantitative Data

  • Methodological Planning, Sampling Design, and Environmental Data Analysis Plans

  • Development of Technical Terms of Reference (TdR/TdRT) for Environmental Data Planning, Processing, and Critical Analysis

  • Methodological Design for Socio-environmental Projects

Environmental studies promoting biodiversity conservation and human well-being
